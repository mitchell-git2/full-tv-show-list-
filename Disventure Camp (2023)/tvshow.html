14 Teens spend the night at a rundown summer camp trying to survive for 1 million dollars.

1x1 - Meet Me in Tipiskaw
Miles away from civilization, surrounded by a unexplored forest, 14 participants of different ages will compete for the prize of one million dollars. The winning team would always win some camping gear for helping them through days and nights, surviving multiple disasters until the finale.
1x2 - Logged & Loaded
The second team challenge is about to commence, and the first elimination starts here. Both teams have to cut ropes using machetes to drop down a set of three different sizes of logs. They then have to bring them to the bottom of the hill by riding on the logs. While riding down, each member on each log will have to grab one flag each.
1x3 - Mazed and Confused
To win, each player from each team must face their fears within a given set of time. Once that player has faced their fear, their team advances to the next room. However, if the player refuses to do their fear, their team has to wait a 30-minute penalty to move on to the next room.
1x4 - Slip N Slide
For this challenge, both teams must reach the floating platform across the waters by using the canoes that the hosts have provided for them. The first team to get all their members to the platform will earn an advantage for the second part of the challenge. In the second part of the challenge, their objective is to knock down each team member's opponent from the opposing team off the platform.
1x5 - Baking My Heart!
For their fifth challenge, the teams are tasked with cooking a set dish for their team members, with a list of disgusting ingredients they are required to use, with two members acting as the cooks and three members acting as the gatherers. Additionally, the winning team will get to choose a representative for the losing's teams elimination, eliminating an extra person from the competition.
1x6 - The Most Tranquilous Game
At this point in the competition, their challenges are only getting harder. Their challenge is aiming for immunity where only one member of each team can ring the bell by the lake. However, they must get to the platform without getting shot with tranquillizers by Jensen.
1x7 - Spit it Out
After a false double elimination, the two latest eliminated players switch teams. For this challenge, one member from each team will be strapped onto a water wheel where they'll collect water using their mouths and spit it into a glass container. Once they've filled it, one player from each team will guide their blindfolded team members to gather large blocks to then assemble them into a picture of their team logos.
1x8 - Questionnaire, if you Dare!
After the morning announcement, the merge has finally come. No more teams means only one of them can win immunity. For their first individual challenge, they're going to answer a set of questions from the phones that the host provided for them. After choosing, the host will ask them who they thought the majority voted for for each question. If they answer correctly, they'll have the option to remove a life from someone. Each player has three lives, and if they lose all lives the cages above them will fall, eliminating them from the challenge.
1x9 - Don't Open, Campers Inside
With 7 players remaining fighting for the million, the show just got abandoned by a zombie apocalypse. The contestants will have to survive this apocalypse for weeks, months, or even years to keep themselves alive. However, it turns out the apocalypse was just a VR simulation set up by Derek and Trevor. The last player standing will win immunity for tonight's elimination.
1x10 - Medal of Dis-honor
Things are a lot harder from here on out. Tonight's challenge is to complete a set of six obstacle courses to retrieve six medallions. They are allowed to pick the order of the obstacles. However, they also have the option to find a hidden immunity totem. The player who wins the challenge will be secured a spot in the final 5.
1x11 - Lights Out
As the finale is right around the corner, the final 5 are faced off with a terrifying challenge. Their 11th challenge is to navigate through an underground cave and find the exit with limited resources. They are however not alone, as a gigantic scorpion is inhabiting the cave they have to avoid. The first one that finds the exit wins immunity and secures a spot in the final 4.
1x12 - Trials & Trip-ulations
The semifinals are here, and the final 4 are split into two teams for the challenges throughout the day, from cliff diving to a test of endurance, with the winning team securing a spot in the final 3, as well as choosing to send someone from the losing team home.
1x13 - A Hearts Desire
The finale is here, and the three finalists are hungry for the prize. Who will be the winner who will go through the two-part challenge and receive the long-awaited prize of One Million Dollars?
