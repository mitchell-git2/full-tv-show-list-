Follow South Africa’s first forensic and investigative psychologist, Micki Pistorius (played by Charlotte Hope), as she studies the minds of South Africa's serial killers. Catch Me A Killer is an adaptation of Micki's autobiography that details her work as the first female criminal profiler in the country, which is brought to life as the events are depicted in the series.

1x1 - The Station Strangler (1)
An enthralling premiere of this South African crime thriller. Set in Mitchell's Plain, 1994, a young man goes missing, and the police approach psychologist Dr Micki Pistorius to help with the case.
1x2 - The Station Strangler (2)
Chris and AJ arrest the man who has been writing the letters to the press. Micki attends the post-mortem of Joey Adamse - her first; and the case is taking its toll on AJ.
1x3 - Cleveland
In Pretoria, Micki meets Brigadier Brink, who puts her onto a new case: another body has just been found in the Cleveland mine dumps.
1x4 - The ABC Killer
Micki and Erika are off to work on a new case: fifteen dead women are found in Atteridgeville. But almost immediately, the team are called, as a new body is discovered at a horrifying scene.
1x5 - Donnybrook
Micki is summoned to the small town of Donnybrook to assist Detectives Nick and Solomon in trying to get a confession out of a suspect in the next forty-eight hours before he appears in court.
1x6 - The Cape Town Prostitute Killer
Micki returns to Cape Town to work on a new case. A serial killer targets prostitutes. A potential suspect emerges, but extracting a confession becomes a race against time.
1x7 - Stewart Wilken

1x8 - The Phoenix Strangler (1)
