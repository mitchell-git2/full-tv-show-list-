After 7 seasons of Total Drama, it's time to go back to the Island! Taking place in the run-down Camp Wawanakwa, contestants must give it their all as they compete in extreme baking, TikToking, drag racing and "disaster and horror movie inspired challenges" for a chance to win the one million dollar prize!

1x1 - Meet the Victims
The Ferocious Trout and The Frogs of Death are the two new teams in an all-new season of Total Drama. Can these newbies battle their way through this dangerous world navigated by their deranged host, Chris McLean? Let's find out.
1x2 - Pirates of the Cabbage'an
The two teams go head-to-head in a pirate cabbage-cannon challenge with Ripper and Axel fighting for leadership and elimination lurking around the corner.
1x3 - Drown Town Abbey
When Damien and Julia are locked inside hyperbaric chambers that slowly fill with water, their teams must go through a dangerous obstacle course to retrieve a code and set them free.
1x4 - Numbskull Island
In this vicious edition of Capture the Flag, the two teams must go against each other to grab the other's skull, while hiding behind shields and weapons.
1x5 - Jurassic Fart
After a putrid baked-beans filled breakfast, the campers must stay hidden from dinosaurs who roam the camp - and they hear everything. Can they control their butts to save their lives and their chances at a million dollars?
1x6 - The Launchback of Notre Game
The teams are catapulted - (via slingshot) in hopes to grab as many points as they can to stay another week at Camp Wawanakwa.
1x7 - Severe Eggs and Pains
The teams have merged and it's up to them to make it to one end of the camp to the other - while staying away from a vicious bird on the loose.
1x8 - The Wheel of Vomit
If the remaining campers want to win immunity, they'll have to stuff as much disgusting food that Chef created into their mouths - without puking or resisting.
1x9 - Paddle Field Earth
A visit to Honey Mountain is on the agenda for this week's challenge.
1x10 - The Truth, the Pole Truth, and Nothing But the Truth
Stay on a pole longer than the other competitors while resisting temptations and rewards is all the remaining campers have to do this week.
1x11 - Tortoise Rigamortis
The way to win immunity this time is by taking as many selfies as you can with animals.
1x12 - Caved by the Bell
The final four travel into the deep dark caves of Camp Wawanakwa to find "TDI tokens".
1x13 - Magma Cum Laude
The final three face the final challenges; only one can win. Who will it be?
